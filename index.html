About AI4SG Website

Welcome to our website dedicated to AI for Social Good, a growing field that applies artificial intelligence to tackle some of the world's biggest social and environmental challenges. Through this website, we aim to showcase the latest developments in AI for Social Good, highlight its importance and impact, and provide resources and information for those who want to learn more and get involved.

Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to perform tasks that typically require human cognitive abilities, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation. AI systems can be classified into three main categories.
